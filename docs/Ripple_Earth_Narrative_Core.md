# Ripple Earth — Narrative Core / 叙事核心

> **A honest document about why we're building this, what it means, and how it fits together.**
> **一份关于我们为什么建造它、它意味着什么、以及各部分如何组合的真实文档。**

---

## 1. Origin / 原点

Two sophomores. We stumbled into this field by accident. Started reading. Started finding things that made us say "wait, that's happening?" One thing led to another. Before we knew it, we had a collection of stories that changed how we see the planet.

两个大二学生。偶然撞进这个领域。开始读。开始发现一些让我们说"等等，这在发生？"的东西。一件事引向另一件。不知不觉间，我们已经有了一堆改变了我们看待地球方式的故事。

We didn't set out to build a company. We set out to **show people what we found.**

我们不是要开公司。我们是要**让别人看到我们发现了什么。**

---

## 2. The Core Thesis / 叙事核心

**The Earth is full of things you didn't know. We saw some of them. Now we want you to see them too.**

**地球上有太多你不知道的事。我们看到了其中一些。现在想让你也看到。**

That's it. It's not a mission statement. It's not a vision. It's simply why this project exists.

就这么多。不是使命宣言。不是愿景。就是这个项目存在的原因。

Every story in Ripple Earth follows the same arc:

Ripple Earth 的每个故事都遵循同样的弧线：

> **Look at the world you know. → Now look again. → There's something you didn't see.**
> **看着你熟悉的世界。→ 再看一次。→ 有你没看到的东西。**

This is not about guilt. It's not about fear. It's about **discovery**. The feeling of learning something that changes how you see everything else.

这不是关于罪恶感。不是关于恐惧。这是关于**发现**。那种学到某件能改变你看待一切的方式的事情时的感觉。

---

## 3. What We're Building / 我们在建什么

A digital Earth that reveals hidden connections. Not a dashboard. Not a textbook. A **revelation engine.**

一个揭示隐藏连接的数字地球。不是一个仪表盘。不是一本教科书。一个**揭示引擎**。

The entry point is a story about 28,000 rubber ducks that fell off a ship in 1992 and spent 15 years tracing ocean currents. A child's bath toy became a scientific tool. That's the Ripple Earth feeling in one story.

入口是一个关于28,000只在1992年掉落的橡皮鸭花了15年追踪洋流的故事。一个孩子的浴缸玩具变成了科学工具。这就是Ripple Earth的感觉——一个故事里。

From there, the stories branch out. Each one connected to the next. Like a knowledge graph you can explore.

从那里，故事分支出去。每个连接到下一个。像一个你可以探索的知识图谱。

---

## 4. The Bridge Model / 桥梁模型

We're not selling to users. We're sitting in the middle.

我们不是向用户收费。我们坐在中间。

`
[Large Organizations / 大型机构]
NASA, NOAA, WWF, UNEP, research labs
├── They have data, research, reports
├── They can't reach young audiences
└── 它们有数据、研究、报告，但触达不到年轻受众
          │
          ▼
    [Ripple Earth — the bridge / 桥梁]
    ├── Takes data from orgs / 从机构拿数据
    ├── Turns it into stories + visuals / 变成故事+视觉
    └── Makes it accessible / 让它可触及
          │
          ▼
[Users / 用户]                    [Enterprises / 企业 + Media / 媒体]
├── See hidden stories            ├── Need environmental content
├── Get the "I had no idea" moment├── Their own content feels fake
└── Share / 分享                  └── Ripple Earth is real
`

**How it sustains itself / 如何维持：**

| Who / 谁 | Gives / 给 | Gets / 得 |
|---|---|---|
| Research orgs / 研究机构 | Data, scientific backing / 数据、科学背书 | Public reach, young audience / 公众触达、年轻受众 |
| Enterprises / 企业 | Sponsorship, server costs / 赞助、服务器费用 | Real environmental content, brand association / 真实环境内容、品牌关联 |
| Users / 用户 | Attention, sharing / 注意力、传播 | New perspectives, social capital / 新视角、社交资本 |
| Us / 我们 | Curation, dev, design / 策展、开发、设计 | Costs covered, audience, network / 成本覆盖、受众、网络 |

This is not a startup. This is a **commons.** Everyone contributes what they can, everyone takes what they need.

这不是一个创业公司。这是一个**公地。** 每个人贡献他们能贡献的，每个人取他们需要的。

---

## 5. The Hidden Story Engine / 隐藏故事引擎

The core asset isn't the website. It's the **knowledge graph** — a growing network of hidden connection stories, each one linked to the next.

核心资产不是网站。是**知识图谱**——一个不断增长的隐藏连接故事网络，每一个都连接到下一个。

`
Sources / 来源               Pipeline / 管线                    Output / 输出
Science journals / 学术期刊  →  NLP extraction / NLP提取        →  Story nodes / 故事节点
NASA/NOAA press / 新闻      →  Surprise scoring / 震惊度评分   →  Knowledge graph / 知识图谱
Environmental news / 环保新闻 →  Visual method matching / 视觉匹配→  Website / 网站
Research databases / 研究数据库 →  Connection linking / 连接关联→  API / 接口
`

Each story is structured as a node with bi-directional links. The duck story connects to ocean currents, which connects to plastic, which connects to the whale pump, which connects to ocean productivity. The graph grows organically.

每个故事都被结构化为一个带双向链接的节点。鸭子的故事连接到洋流，洋流连接到塑料，塑料连接到鲸鱼泵，鲸鱼泵连接到海洋生产力。图谱有机地生长。

---

## 6. Why This Matters / 为什么这重要

Environmental storytelling is stuck.

环保叙事卡住了。

Same images. Same graphs. Same guilt trips. People have tuned out. They know the problem exists, but they can't feel it. The scale is too large, the connection too abstract.

同样的图片。同样的图表。同样的负罪感。人们已经关掉了。他们知道问题存在，但感受不到。尺度太大，连接太抽象。

Ripple Earth doesn't show them what they already know. It shows them what they've never seen. And once you see it, you can't unsee it.

Ripple Earth 不是展示他们已经知道的东西。它展示他们从未见过的东西。一旦你看到了，你就无法看不见。

> **You cannot protect what you cannot see.**
> **你无法保护你看不见的东西。**

---

## 7. Our Position / 我们的位置

Two sophomores. Not experts. Not entrepreneurs. Just people who found something interesting and wanted to share it.

两个大二学生。不是专家。不是企业家。只是发现了有趣的事情并想分享的人。

That's our advantage. We're not selling anything. We're not trying to convince anyone of anything. We're just **showing what we found.** The honesty of that position is itself the message.

这是我们的优势。我们不是在卖任何东西。我们不是想说服任何人任何事情。我们只是在**展示我们发现的。** 这个立场的诚实本身就是信息。
