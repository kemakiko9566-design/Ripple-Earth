### 1.1 The Whale Pump / 鲸鱼泵——动物作为海洋工程师

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Before industrial whaling, there were roughly 1 million blue whales in the Southern Ocean. Today: approximately 10,000. But whales are not just victims of history — they are ecosystem engineers. Whales feed at depth and release nutrient-rich fecal plumes at the surface, fertilizing phytoplankton blooms on a massive scale. This is called the **whale pump**. A single whale cycles more nutrients vertically than any physical ocean process. Before whaling, this pump sustained a significant portion of Southern Ocean productivity.

在工业捕鲸之前，南大洋大约有100万头蓝鲸。今天：大约1万头。但鲸鱼不仅仅是历史的受害者——它们是生态系统的工程师。鲸鱼在深水觅食，在水面释放富含营养的排泄物，大规模地滋养浮游植物。这就是**鲸鱼泵**。一头鲸鱼垂直循环的养分量超过任何物理海洋过程。在捕鲸之前，这个泵支撑了南大洋生产力的重要部分。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

Most people think of whales as passive victims. They do not know whales actively fertilize the ocean. The recovery of whale populations is not just a conservation success — it is restoring a planetary-scale nutrient cycle that we barely understand.

大多数人认为鲸鱼是被动的受害者。他们不知道鲸鱼在主动"施肥"海洋。鲸鱼种群的恢复不仅仅是一个保护成功的案例——它在修复一个我们几乎不了解的行星级营养循环。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

A single whale diving and surfacing, with a glowing nutrient plume expanding at the surface — then multiplying to an entire population. Switch between "whale pump active" and "whale pump removed" views showing ocean productivity collapse.

一头鲸鱼下潜和上升，水面上一股发光的营养羽流扩散开来——然后乘以整个种群。在"鲸鱼泵开启"和"鲸鱼泵移除"两种视图之间切换，展示海洋生产力的崩溃。

**Data / 数据：** Whale population estimates (historical vs current), chlorophyll concentration in feeding grounds, whale migration tracks

### 1.2 The Concrete Paradox / 混凝土悖论——你每天走过的二氧化碳吸收器

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Cement production accounts for roughly 8% of global CO2 emissions. But there is a second story that almost nobody knows: concrete absorbs CO2 over its lifetime through a process called **carbonation**. Estimates suggest that over its lifecycle, concrete re-absorbs roughly 30% of the emissions from cement production. Every sidewalk, every building, every bridge is quietly pulling CO2 from the atmosphere.

水泥生产约占全球CO₂排放量的8%。但还有一个几乎没人知道的第二故事：混凝土在其生命周期中通过一种叫做**碳化**的过程吸收CO₂。估算表明，在其整个生命周期中，混凝土重新吸收约30%的水泥生产排放。每一条人行道、每一栋建筑、每一座桥都在悄悄地从大气中拉回CO₂。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

The material most associated with carbon emissions is also a carbon sink — just on a much slower timescale. The hidden accounting of concrete is an object lesson in how systems have effects we do not see.

那个与碳排放关联最深的材料同时也是一种碳汇——只是在更慢的时间尺度上。混凝土的隐藏账户是一个关于系统如何产生我们看不见的效果的实物课。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Show a city — buildings, sidewalks, bridges — slowly glowing with absorbed carbon. Show the CO2 being pulled from the air into concrete surfaces. Contrast with the emission plume from the cement factory.

展示一座城市——建筑、人行道、桥梁——慢慢地因吸收碳而发光。展示CO₂从空气中被拉入混凝土表面的过程。与水泥厂的排放羽流形成对比。

**Data / 数据：** Global cement production vs carbonation rates (Lombardi Net Zero Tracker, Nature 2023)

### 1.3 The Plastic Sky / 塑料天空——50年前不存在的循环

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Microplastics are not just in the ocean. They are in the air. They have been found in the Pyrenees mountains, in Arctic snow, in the rain falling over the United Kingdom. Microplastics have been detected in human blood, lungs, placentas, breast milk. The atmosphere has become a **microplastic highway** — particles cycle from ocean to air to land to rain to soil to ocean again. This is a new planetary cycle that simply did not exist before the 1950s.

微塑料不仅在海里。它们在空气中。人们在比利牛斯山脉、北极的雪、英国的雨中都发现了它们。微塑料已经在人类血液、肺、胎盘、母乳中被检测到。大气层已经变成了一条**微塑料高速公路**——颗粒从海洋到空气到陆地到雨水到土壤再到海洋循环。这是一个在1950年代之前根本不存在的新的行星级循环。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

Plastic pollution is usually shown as garbage patches and turtle entanglements. The idea that plastic is falling from the sky — that every breath contains microscopic plastic — is viscerally disturbing and unknown to most people.

塑料污染通常被展示为垃圾带和被缠绕的海龟。塑料从天而降——每一次呼吸都含有微观塑料——这个想法在直觉上令人不安，而大多数人都不知道。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Globe view with atmospheric particle system. Particles rise from ocean gyres, are carried by wind systems, and descend as rain on land. Show microplastic accumulation in rainfall over time (1960s = none, 2020s = everywhere).

带大气粒子系统的地球视图。颗粒从海洋环流中升起，被风系统携带，以雨的形式降落在陆地上。展示雨水中微塑料随时间的积累（1960年代=无，2020年代=无处不在）。

**Data / 数据：** Atmospheric microplastic deposition studies (Nature 2019-2024), global atmospheric transport models

### 1.4 The Ozone Success / 臭氧成功——没人庆祝的条约

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

The Montreal Protocol (1987) is the single most successful environmental treaty in history. It phased out CFCs. The ozone hole is healing — projected to fully close by 2060. But fewer people know: (1) CFCs were also powerful greenhouse gases — their phase-out has delayed global warming by roughly a decade. (2) The ozone hole has physically shifted Southern Hemisphere weather patterns — the jet stream moved, rainfall in Australia and South America changed, ocean currents altered.

蒙特利尔议定书（1987）是历史上最成功的环境条约。它淘汰了CFCs。臭氧层空洞正在愈合——预计到2060年完全关闭。但很少有人知道：(1) CFCs也是强效温室气体——它们的淘汰使全球变暖推迟了大约十年。(2) 臭氧空洞实际上改变了南半球的天气模式——急流移动了，澳大利亚和南美洲的降雨改变了，洋流也发生了变化。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

People either do not know about the ozone success, or they remember it as a "solved" 1990s problem. Almost nobody knows it changed Southern Hemisphere weather. And almost nobody credits CFC phase-out as a massive climate win.

人们要么不知道臭氧空洞的成功，要么把它当作一个1990年代"解决了的"问题。几乎没有人知道它改变了南半球的天气。几乎没有人把CFC的淘汰当作一个巨大的气候成就。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Globe showing the ozone hole as a pulsing void over Antarctica. Time-lapse: hole expands, then slowly heals. Show weather pattern shift (jet stream movement) linked to hole expansion.

展示南极上空搏动的虚空——臭氧空洞。时间推移：空洞扩大，然后慢慢愈合。展示与空洞扩大相关的天气模式变化（急流移动）。

**Data / 数据：** NASA Ozone Watch, NOAA climate modeling of ozone-weather interaction

### 1.5 The Sound of Silence / 寂静之声——海洋噪音污染

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Before industrialization, the ocean was filled with biological sounds: whale songs, fish calls, shrimp clicks, seal barks. Today, shipping noise has raised the ocean's background sound level by 15-20 decibels since the 1950s. Blue whales have changed the frequency of their calls to be heard over ship noise. Fish migration is disrupted. This is **invisible pollution** — we cannot see it, but it changes the behavior of an entire ecosystem.

在工业化之前，海洋充满了生物的声音：鲸鱼的歌声、鱼类的叫唤、虾的咔嗒声、海豹的吠叫。今天，自1950年代以来，航运噪音使海洋的背景声音水平提高了15-20分贝。蓝鲸不得不改变其叫声的频率以在船舶噪音中被听到。鱼类迁徙被扰乱。这是**无形的污染**——我们看不见，但它改变了整个生态系统的行为。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

People think of pollution as visible — smog, oil spills, plastic. The idea that noise is a global pollutant affecting animals across entire ocean basins is almost completely unknown outside marine biology.

人们认为污染是可见的——烟雾、漏油、塑料。噪音是一种影响整个海盆的全球性污染物——这个概念在海洋生物学之外几乎完全不为人知。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Globe with an audio layer. Ships generate expanding sound circles. Whale calls visualized as organic waveforms that shrink and change frequency as ship noise increases. Compare 1950s ocean vs 2020s ocean.

带音频层的地球。船只产生扩张的声环。鲸鱼叫声被可视化——随着船只噪音增加而收缩和改变频率的有机波形。对比1950年代的海洋和2020年代的海洋。

**Data / 数据：** NOAA Ocean Noise Reference Station network, AIS shipping density, whale call frequency shift studies

### 1.6 Arctic Zombie Fires / 北极僵尸火灾——熬过冬天的火

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

In the Arctic, some wildfires do not fully die in autumn. They smolder underground in peat layers, burning through the winter, and re-emerge the following spring. These are called **zombie fires** or overwintering fires. This phenomenon was virtually unknown before the 2000s. As the Arctic warms, zombie fires become more frequent. They release ancient carbon stored in peat for thousands of years — carbon that has not seen the atmosphere since the last ice age.

在北极，有些野火在秋天不会完全熄灭。它们在地下泥炭层中阴燃，烧过整个冬天，在来年春天重新出现。这被称为**僵尸火灾**或越冬火灾。在2000年代之前，这种现象几乎不为人知。随着北极变暖，僵尸火灾越来越频繁。它们释放了在泥炭中储存了数千年的古老碳——那些自上个冰河时代以来就没有接触过大气层的碳。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

"Zombie fire" is inherently gripping. The idea that fire can survive an Arctic winter underground, invisible, and re-emerge is counterintuitive. The connection between ancient carbon and modern fire is a gut-level climate feedback.

"僵尸火灾"本身就引人入胜。火可以在北极的地下渡过冬天，看不见，然后重新出现——这个想法是反直觉的。古老碳与现代火之间的联系是一种本能的、触及内心的气候反馈。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Cross-section view of Arctic landscape. Fire burns above ground, then sinks into peat. Surface snow falls, but fire continues underground as a dim orange glow. Spring: fire re-emerges as new flames burst through melting snow.

北极景观的横截面视图。火在地面燃烧，然后沉入泥炭。地表雪落，但火在地下继续燃烧，发出暗橙色的光。春天：火焰冲破融雪重新出现。

**Data / 数据：** NASA FIRMS, Canadian Forest Service zombie fire tracking, peat carbon content studies

### 1.7 The Dark Fleet / 黑暗舰队——隐藏于众目睽睽之下的船

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Hundreds of fishing vessels and oil tankers turn off their AIS transmitters (the system that broadcasts their location) to hide. Global Fishing Watch uses satellite radar to detect these "dark" vessels — ships that are physically there but invisible to conventional tracking systems. Approximately 1 in 5 fishing vessels in some regions operates illegally or unreported.

数百艘渔船和油轮关闭了AIS发射器（广播其位置的系统）以隐藏自己。Global Fishing Watch使用卫星雷达来检测这些"黑暗"船只——物理上存在但对传统追踪系统不可见的船。在一些地区，大约五分之一渔船进行非法或未报告的作业。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

The idea of an invisible network of ships — a fleet that exists but is deliberately hidden — feels like a spy thriller. The fact that satellite technology can "see" them even when they hide creates a fascinating cat-and-mouse narrative.

一支无形的船队——存在但故意隐藏——这个想法感觉像是间谍惊悚片。即使它们隐藏，卫星技术也能"看到"它们——这个事实创造了一个引人入胜的猫鼠叙事。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Globe with two layers: official AIS tracks (bright, continuous lines) and dark fleet detected by radar (glowing red dots appearing when AIS is turned off). Show a vessel go "dark."

带两层数据的地球：官方AIS航线（明亮的连续线）和被雷达探测到的黑暗舰队（AIS关闭时出现的红色发光点）。展示一艘船"消失"的过程。

**Data / 数据：** Global Fishing Watch dark fleet detection, satellite SAR (Synthetic Aperture Radar)

### 1.8 The Sleeping Giant / 沉睡的巨人——永久冻土融化

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Permafrost covers 25% of the Northern Hemisphere land surface. It holds roughly **1,500 billion tons of organic carbon** — twice the amount currently in the atmosphere. As permafrost thaws, microbes decompose that organic matter, releasing CO2 and methane. This is already happening — Arctic permafrost releases an estimated 300-600 million tons of carbon per year. If it thaws at scale, it could add 0.3-1.0 degree C to global warming beyond human contributions. It also releases ancient pathogens: in 2016, a child died from anthrax in Siberia, linked to a reindeer carcass exposed by thawing permafrost.

永久冻土覆盖了北半球陆地表面的25%。它储存了大约**1.5万亿吨有机碳**——是当前大气中碳含量的两倍。随着永久冻土融化，微生物分解这些有机物，释放出CO₂和甲烷。这已经在发生——北极永久冻土每年释放约3-6亿吨碳。如果它大规模融化，可能使全球变暖在人类贡献之外再增加0.3-1.0°C。它还释放古老病原体：2016年，西伯利亚一名儿童死于炭疽，与一具因永久冻土融化而暴露的驯鹿尸体有关。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

The scale of carbon stored in frozen ground is larger than most people grasp. The self-reinforcing feedback (warming → thaw → more carbon → more warming) is the most consequential feedback loop most people have never heard of.

冷冻土壤中储存的碳的规模超出了大多数人的认知。自我强化的反馈（变暖→融化→更多碳→更暖）是大多数人从未听说过的最重要的反馈循环。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Globe with permafrost overlay. As temperature rises, permafrost shrinks — carbon release zone expands as a glowing red area. Cross-section: frozen soil → thawed → microbial activity → CO2/methane bubbles rising.

带永久冻土覆盖的地球。随着温度上升，永久冻土缩小——碳释放区作为一个发光的红色区域扩大。横截面：冻土→解冻土→微生物活动→CO₂/甲烷气泡上升。

**Data / 数据：** NSIDC permafrost monitoring, NASA ABoVE, IPCC permafrost carbon feedback estimates

### 1.9 Forest Breathing / 森林呼吸——从太空测量的行星脉搏

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Satellites measure the Earth's breath. Every year, as spring arrives in the Northern Hemisphere, the entire land surface turns green. The biosphere takes in CO2 at a massive scale. The Keeling Curve shows this as a sawtooth pattern — the annual oscillation of the Earth's lungs. The amplitude of this oscillation has increased by 50% since 1960, meaning the biosphere is "breathing harder" — both greening and stress.

卫星在测量地球的呼吸。每年，当春天到达北半球时，整个陆地表面变绿。生物圈大规模吸收CO₂。基林曲线将其显示为锯齿状图案——地球肺部的年度振荡。这种振荡的幅度自1960年以来增加了50%，意味着生物圈在"更用力地呼吸"——既是绿化，也是压力。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

The idea that the entire planet has a measurable breath — a pulse visible in a graph, driven by every plant on Earth — is almost poetic. Most people see only the upward CO2 trend; they miss the living pulse beneath it.

整个地球有一个可测量的呼吸——一个由地球上每一棵植物驱动的、在图表中可见的脉搏——这个想法几乎是诗意的。大多数人只看到CO₂的上升趋势；他们没有看到下面跳动的脉搏。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Globe with a "breathing" overlay: green pulse sweeps across continents seasonally. The atmosphere visibly contracts and expands with the seasonal CO2 oscillation. Keeling Curve animated alongside the globe.

带"呼吸"覆盖层的地球：绿色脉冲随季节扫过各大洲。大气层随着季节性CO₂振荡而明显收缩和扩张。基林曲线与地球一起动画播放。

**Data / 数据：** Keeling Curve (NOAA/Scripps), NASA MODIS NDVI, OCO-2 satellite CO2 column

### 1.10 The Sargasso Enigma / 马尾藻之谜——从太空可见的突然海藻带

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Before 2011, the Sargasso Sea had a natural amount of floating sargassum seaweed. Then something changed. Beginning in 2011, a massive belt of sargassum began forming in the tropical Atlantic — stretching from West Africa to Brazil to the Caribbean. The **Great Atlantic Sargassum Belt** is now visible from space. The cause: deforestation in the Amazon (increasing nutrient runoff) combined with warming ocean temperatures.

2011年之前，马尾藻海有自然数量的漂浮马尾藻。然后事情变了。从2011年开始，一条巨大的马尾藻带开始在热带大西洋形成——从西非延伸到巴西再到加勒比海。**大西洋马尾藻带**现在从太空中可见。原因：亚马逊森林砍伐（增加营养径流）与海洋温度升高相结合。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

A new ecosystem appears in the ocean, visible from space, within a human lifetime. The cause links Amazon deforestation to Caribbean seaweed — a trans-Atlantic connection most people would never make.

一个新的生态系统在海洋中出现，从太空中可见，发生在一个人的一生之内。原因将亚马逊森林砍伐与加勒比海藻联系起来——大多数人不可能会做出的跨大西洋连接。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

Globe zoomed to the tropical Atlantic. A green-gold belt appears and expands year by year (2011-present). Show nutrient flow from Amazon river meeting ocean currents.

地球放大到热带大西洋。一条绿金色带子年复一年地出现和扩大（2011年至今）。展示来自亚马逊河的养分流与洋流汇合。

**Data / 数据：** University of South Florida Sargassum Watch, NASA MODIS ocean color

### 1.11 Trees Making Smog / 树木制造烟雾——城市VOC悖论

**The Hidden Story / 隐藏的故事：**

Trees emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs) — especially isoprene. In rural areas, this is harmless. But in cities, VOCs from trees combine with NOx from cars in sunlight to form ground-level ozone — the main component of smog. Planting trees in urban areas with high traffic can actually worsen air quality. The chemistry is more complex than "trees = good."

树木释放挥发性有机化合物（VOCs）——尤其是异戊二烯。在乡村地区，这是无害的。但在城市中，树木的VOCs与汽车的NOx在阳光下结合，形成地面臭氧——烟雾的主要成分。在交通量大的城市地区植树实际上可能恶化空气质量。化学比"树=好"更复杂。

**The Surprise Factor / 震惊点：**

The idea that trees — the symbol of environmental purity — can contribute to smog is deeply counterintuitive. It forces the audience to reconsider simple narratives.

树木——环境纯净的象征——会助长烟雾——这个想法是深深反直觉的。它迫使观众重新审视简单的叙事。

**Visual Opportunity / 视觉方案：**

City view with two chemical layers: NOx from cars (red particles), VOCs from trees (green particles). In sunlight, they combine into ozone (orange particles) that fills the urban air.

带两个化学层的城市视图：汽车NOx（红色颗粒），树木VOC（绿色颗粒）。在阳光下，它们结合成填充城市空气的臭氧（橙色颗粒）。

**Data / 数据：** EPA air quality monitoring, urban VOC studies, TROPOMI satellite NO2 data

# 2. Projects That Reveal the Hidden / 揭示隐藏的项目

Case studies of projects specifically designed to uncover, visualize, and communicate underappreciated or invisible Earth system phenomena.
专门为了揭示、可视化和传播被忽视或不可见的地球系统现象而设计的项目案例研究。

## 2.1 Global Fishing Watch / 揭露黑暗舰队

- **Link:** https://globalfishingwatch.org
- **What It Reveals / 揭示什么:** Ships that turn off their location transmitters to hide illegal activity / 关闭位置发射器以隐藏非法活动的船只
- **Method / 方法:** Satellite radar (SAR) detects vessels regardless of AIS status / 卫星雷达检测船只，无论AIS状态如何
- **Discovery Impact / 发现冲击:** ~1 in 5 fishing vessels in some regions operate in the dark / 部分地区约1/5的渔船在黑暗中作业
- **Visual Form / 视觉形式:** Heatmap of vessel activity, dual-layer comparison (AIS vs radar) / 船只活动热力图，双层对比（AIS vs 雷达）
- **Relevance to Ripple Earth / 相关性:** Revealing invisible human activity through satellite data is exactly the kind of hidden layer the project needs / 通过卫星数据揭示不可见的人类活动正是项目需要的隐藏图层

## 2.2 MethaneSAT / TROPOMI / 看见不可见的温室气体

- **Link:** https://www.methanesat.org / https://www.tropomi.eu
- **What It Reveals:** Methane plumes invisible to the naked eye but visible in specific spectral bands / 肉眼不可见但在特定光谱波段可见的甲烷羽流
- **Method:** Hyperspectral imaging detects methane absorption bands / 高光谱成像检测甲烷吸收带
- **Discovery Impact:** Methane super-emitters are far more common than reported. A single leak visible from space / 甲烷超级排放源比报道的常见得多。单个泄漏从太空可见
- **Visual Form:** Methane plumes overlaid on globe, color-coded by concentration / 地球上的甲烷羽流叠加，按浓度着色
- **Relevance:** The "make the invisible visible" mechanic is central to Ripple Earth / "让不可见变得可见"的机制是Ripple Earth的核心

---

## 2.3 NASA OCO-2 / 绘制行星的呼吸

- **Link:** https://ocov2.jpl.nasa.gov
- **What It Reveals:** Global distribution of CO2 — where emissions occur AND where the biosphere absorbs carbon / CO₂的全球分布——排放发生的地方和生物圈吸收碳的地方
- **Method:** Spectrometer measuring atmospheric CO2 column from space / 从太空测量大气CO₂柱浓度的光谱仪
- **Discovery Impact:** Shows the seasonal breathing of the biosphere — the planetary pulse visualized / 展示生物圈的季节性呼吸——可视化了的行星脉搏
- **Visual Form:** Animated globe with CO2 concentration as a pulsing color layer / CO₂浓度作为搏动颜色层的动画地球
- **Relevance:** The biological pulse of the planet is a perfect Ripple Earth narrative / 行星的生物脉搏是一个完美的Ripple Earth叙事

---

## 2.4 NASA GRACE / 隐藏的水危机

- **Link:** https://grace.jpl.nasa.gov
- **What It Reveals:** Groundwater depletion invisible from the surface. Measures changes in Earth's gravity field as aquifers are depleted / 从地表不可见的地下水枯竭。测量地下水含水层枯竭时地球引力场的变化
- **Method:** Twin satellites measure minute gravitational differences caused by water mass changes / 双卫星测量由水质量变化引起的微小引力差异
- **Discovery Impact:** Revealed that the world's major aquifers are being depleted at rates far exceeding recharge / 揭示了世界主要含水层正在以远超补给的速度枯竭
- **Visual Form:** Globe with gravity anomaly color map showing regions where groundwater is disappearing / 带引力异常彩色地图的地球，显示地下水消失的区域
- **Relevance:** A hidden crisis happening beneath our feet, measurable only through gravity / 一个发生在我们脚下的隐藏危机，只能通过引力来测量

---

## 2.5 NASA VIIRS Nightlights / 人类足迹

- **Link:** https://earthobservatory.nasa.gov/features/NightLights
- **What It Reveals:** The true extent of human presence on Earth at night / 夜间人类在地球上存在的真实范围
- **Method:** Day-night band detects extremely low levels of visible light / 昼夜波段检测极低水平的可见光
- **Discovery Impact:** Shows urbanization spread, fishing fleets at sea, gas flares, and the ecological impact zone of artificial light / 展示城市化蔓延、海上捕鱼船队、天然气火炬和人工光的生态影响区域
- **Visual Form:** Globe at night with emissive glow overlay / 带发光覆盖层的夜间地球
- **Relevance:** The most direct visual of "human presence on the planet" / "人类在地球上的存在"的最直接视觉表现

---

## 2.6 NOAA Ocean Noise / 声学海洋

- **Link:** https://www.pmel.noaa.gov/acoustics
- **What It Reveals:** The changing soundscape of the ocean — anthropogenic noise pollution / 海洋声景的变化——人为噪音污染
- **Method:** Hydrophone arrays across the Pacific and Atlantic continuously record ocean sound / 横跨太平洋和大西洋的水听器阵列持续记录海洋声音
- **Discovery Impact:** Shows dramatic increase in shipping noise, whale call frequency shifts, acoustic migration patterns / 展示航运噪音的急剧增加、鲸鱼叫声频率的变化、声学迁徙模式
- **Visual Form:** Globe with sound intensity overlay, frequency spectrum animation / 带声音强度覆盖层的地球，频谱动画
- **Relevance:** Invisible pollution through sound. A temporal and immersive data layer / 通过声音实现的无形污染。一个时间性和沉浸式的数据层

---

## 2.7 The Plastisphere / 污染诞生的新生态系统

- **Link:** https://science.nasa.gov/earth-science/oceanography/plastisphere
- **What It Reveals:** A new ecological community living on floating plastic in the ocean / 生活在海洋漂浮塑料上的新生态群落
- **Method:** Sampling and DNA sequencing of microbial communities on ocean plastic / 海洋塑料上微生物群落的采样和DNA测序
- **Discovery Impact:** Plastic is not just pollution. It is a new habitat. A synthetic ecosystem that did not exist before the 1950s / 塑料不仅仅是污染。它是一个新的栖息地。一个在1950年代之前不存在的合成生态系统
- **Visual Form:** Microscope-style view of plastic particles with living communities / 显微镜视角：带有生物群落的塑料颗粒
- **Relevance:** Pollution as transformation — the unintended creation of a new ecosystem / 污染作为转化——一个全新生态系统的意外创造

## 6.1 Revelation Mechanics / 揭示机制

| Mechanic / 机制 | Implementation / 实现 | Creates This Feeling / 创造这种感觉 |

| **Spectral reveal / 频谱揭示** | Invisible data rendered in visible color palette, slowly fading in / 不可见数据在可见调色板中渲染，缓慢淡入 | The world contains hidden information / 世界包含隐藏信息 |

| **Time compression / 时间压缩** | GSAP timeline speeding up slow processes / GSAP时间线加速缓慢过程 | Decades of change in 10 seconds / 几十年的变化在10秒内 |
| **Cross-section toggle / 横截面切换** | Camera pans from surface to below surface / 相机从地表摇到地下 | Hidden world in the same geographic space / 同一地理空间中的隐藏世界 |
| **Scale anchor / 尺度锚点** | Familiar object shown alongside the phenomenon / 熟悉物体与现象并列展示 | Makes abstract magnitude feel real / 让抽象的规模感觉真实 |
| **Causal chain animation / 因果链动画** | Animated arrows from cause to effect / 从原因到效果的动画箭头 | Makes invisible system logic visible / 让不可见的系统逻辑变得可见 |

| Ghost layer opacity / 幽灵层透明度 | Three.js ShaderMaterial with uniform opacity / 统一透明度的着色器材质 | Per-layer GPU control / 每层GPU控制 |

## 7.3 Layer Structure / 层结构

`
Layer 7: [Data Panel / 数据面板] Values, sources, narrative text (2D UI)
Layer 6: [Revelation Overlay / 揭示覆盖] Ghost particles, sound waves, causal arrows (additive blend)
Layer 5: [Active Data Layer / 活跃数据层] The current hidden signal (glowing particles, flow lines)
Layer 4: [User Interaction / 用户交互] Cursor, click ripple, hover highlight
Layer 3: [Atmosphere / 大气层] Globe glow, atmospheric scattering
Layer 2: [Baseline Globe / 基线地球] Earth with standard surface (satellite imagery)
Layer 1: [Background / 背景] Deep space / 深空
`

**Key insight / 核心洞察:** Layer 5 (Active Data) starts invisible and is revealed by user action. The default state shows only Layers 1-3. The hidden story is always present but not visible until the viewer discovers it.
第5层（活跃数据层）开始时不可见，通过用户操作揭示。默认状态只显示第1-3层。隐藏的故事始终存在，但在观众发现之前不可见。

## 7.4 The Revelation Moment / 揭示时刻

Animation pattern for every story / 每个故事的动画模式：

1. **Setup / 设置** — Globe shows the baseline state (e.g. clean ocean with ship traffic) / 地球显示基线状态（例如，有船只交通的干净海洋）
2. **Signal introduction / 信号引入** — A single anomalous element appears (a whale dives, nutrient plume blooms) / 一个异常元素出现（鲸鱼下潜，营养羽流绽放）
3. **Scale reveal / 规模揭示** — The single element multiplies to show planetary scale (hundreds of whales, each with a plume) / 单个元素倍增展示行星级规模（数百头鲸鱼，每头都有羽流）
4. **Contrast / 对比** — Counterfactual toggle shows the world without this signal (ocean productivity drops) / 反事实切换展示了没有这个信号的世界（海洋生产力下降）
5. **Data grounding / 数据锚定** — Real data values appear, showing this is not an animation — it is a measurement / 真实数据值出现，表明这不是动画——是测量结果

## Design Manifesto / 设计宣言

> **Ripple Earth is not a dashboard. It is a revelation engine.**
> **Ripple Earth 不是一个仪表盘。它是一个揭示引擎。**
>
> The world is not what it seems. The ocean is not silent. Concrete breathes. Plastic falls from the sky. Whales fertilize forests of plankton. Ships hide in plain sight. Fire survives winter. The ground beneath you holds twice as much carbon as the sky.
> 世界不是表面看起来那样。海洋不是沉默的。混凝土在呼吸。塑料从天而降。鲸鱼在施肥森林般的浮游植物。船只在众目睽睽之下隐藏。火活过了冬天。你脚下的土地中储存的碳是天空中的两倍。
>
> Every story in Ripple Earth follows the same arc: **Look at the world you know. Now look again. There is something you did not see.**
> Ripple Earth 的每一个故事都遵循相同的弧线：**看着你熟悉的世界。现在再看一次。有你没看到的东西。**
>
> The visual language is built for this one purpose: hide the signal, let the viewer discover it, and make that discovery hit like a wave.
> 视觉语言是为这一个目的而建的：隐藏信号，让观众发现它，让那个发现如波浪般冲击。
>
> This is the Ripple. Not the obvious. The overlooked.
> 这就是涟漪。不是显而易见的。是被忽视的。
>
> **Small thing. Hidden connection. Planetary scale.**
> **微小的事物。隐藏的连接。行星级的规模。**
